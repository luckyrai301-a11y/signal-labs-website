Our Work in Action

Project: Sporticos - The Sports Pub

Focus: Competitive Differentiation & PR Strategy

The Challenge:

Sporticos, a sports-themed pub, was performing well but struggled to stand out in a location saturated with high-profile competitors like TBSE and Brew House. They needed a strategy to capture a loyal customer base.

On-Ground Diagnosis & Insight:

Through on-ground PR and observation, we diagnosed that competitors were selling generic "good times," while Sporticos's unique "sports" identity was underutilized. The insight was clear: Don't compete on price or menu; compete on **community**. We identified an opportunity to position Sporticos as the definitive "home ground" for local sports fans.

The Growth (Result):

We prescribed a PR and events strategy focused on creating a fan community (live match screenings, fan club meetups). This differentiated Sporticos from generic lounges, resulting in a **35% increase in footfall on match nights** and a **20% rise in repeat customers**, establishing it as the go-to sports hub in the area.

Our Work in Action

Project: FIFA World Cup 2022, Qatar

Focus: Crowd Management & Fan Experience

The Challenge:

To deliver a world-class fan experience for a global audience within a culturally conservative environment, maximizing engagement and revenue.

On-Ground Diagnosis:

Our on-ground observation revealed a gap between fan expectations for a celebratory football atmosphere and the existing local restrictions. We identified key friction points in crowd movement and a clear demand for more liberal fan zones.

The Growth (Result):

By providing data-backed recommendations, including the strategic approval of alcohol in designated zones, the result was a significant boost in fan satisfaction, positive international press, and record-breaking revenue generation for a host nation.
